Coronavirus what it is?

Coronavirus is a type of virus that can cause respiratory illness in humans, ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as SARS, MERS, and COVID-19. It is highly contagious and spreads through respiratory droplets when an infected person talks, coughs, or sneezes.

COVID-19 Prevention Tips

COVID-19 is a highly contagious respiratory illness caused by a virus that spreads through respiratory droplets. To prevent transmission, maintain distance, wear a mask, wash hands, get vaccinated, and practice respiratory etiquette.

  • Wear a mask: Make sure to wear a mask that covers both your nose and mouth and ensure that it fits snugly against your face.
  • Practice good hand hygiene: Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds or use a hand sanitizer that contains at least 60% alcohol.
  • Social distance: Maintain a distance of at least 6 feet (2 meters) from others and avoid crowded places and large gatherings.
  • Stay home if you're feeling sick: If you're feeling sick or experiencing symptoms of COVID-19, stay home and self-isolate to avoid spreading the virus to others.
  • Get vaccinated: Get vaccinated when it's your turn. The COVID-19 vaccines have been shown to be safe and effective at preventing COVID-19.
  • Follow local guidelines and regulations: Stay informed about the local guidelines and regulations related to COVID-19 in your area and follow them accordingly.
